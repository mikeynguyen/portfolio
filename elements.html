About Me

About the life of a young God.

It's Personal

My name is Mike Nguyen. I am 16 years old. I've been living in San Francisco basically my whole life. I was born in Fresno, but never lived there because it's a ghost town. My parents are Vietnamese islanders that immagrated here and I have one sister that is around 15 years old. I am addicted to video games and most mexican foods. All my life I have always been a well spoken kid that turned shy out of nowhere ever since high school. I used to feel like the king of the world at jokes and making everybody laugh and happy, but I guess being exposed to more class clowns kind of changed me to letting other people have the spotlight, and making me feel more insecure if I let a joke out, because it would look like I tried, because I'm quiet.

Early Life

It wasn't always like this to realize I was a god.

My life took a distinguished route when I joined this program called Youth & Government. This program is basically a model of our legislature and court for the youth to come in and speak their minds, about any bills, or some of the world's biggest problems our topic of discussion. I was kind of a little shy and never spoke at all during my freshman year, and I actually spoke once as my sophomore year. I was only in a room with about 18 kids, but still I was feeling nervous and shakey while speaking because I'm an awkward nigga.
